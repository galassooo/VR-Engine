\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a GPU fragment shader, which processes rasterized fragments to determine their final colors. 

\doxylink{class_eng_1_1_fragment_shader}{Fragment\+Shader} compiles and encapsulates fragment-\/stage GLSL code, used for per-\/pixel operations such as lighting, texturing, and color blending. Inherits base loading, compilation, and rendering interface from \doxylink{class_eng_1_1_shader}{Shader}.
