\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a camera using a perspective projection. 

The \doxylink{class_perspective_camera}{Perspective\+Camera} generates a projection matrix that simulates the effect of a camera lens, with objects appearing smaller as their distance from the camera increases. It inherits from the \doxylink{class_camera}{Camera} class.
