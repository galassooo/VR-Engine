\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a light source that emits light in a specific direction with a cone-\/shaped area of effect. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_spot_light}{Spot\+Light}} simulates a focused light source, such as a flashlight or stage light. It is characterized by its direction, cutoff angle, and falloff factor, which define the shape and intensity distribution of the light beam. It inherits common properties from the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_light}{Light}} base class.
